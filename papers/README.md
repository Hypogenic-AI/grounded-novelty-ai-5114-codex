# Downloaded Papers

This directory contains 16 downloaded PDFs plus chunked page ranges for priority papers in `papers/pages/`.

1. **Can LLMs Generate Novel Research Ideas? A Large-Scale Human Study with 100+ NLP Researchers**
   - File: `2409.04109_llm_research_ideas_human_study.pdf`
   - Authors: Chenglei Si, Diyi Yang, Tatsunori Hashimoto
   - Year: 2024
   - Source: https://arxiv.org/abs/2409.04109
   - Why relevant: controlled expert comparison of human and LLM-generated NLP research ideas; reports higher judged novelty for LLM ideas but weaker feasibility and diversity issues.
